    Bilimetri

    September 2022 - Present
    Ankara, TR

    Bilimetri is a science communication platform maintained by volunteers. The platform is academically based, maintained with the consultancy of scientists and independently audited. Its aim is to reach more people with science, increase curiosity in science and try to remove the obstacles to the inadequate funding of science.

    • Established a team of volunteer writers, editors, and advisors. Actively involved in the planning process to ensure regular content creation and site updates.
    • We publish developments in the scientific community, new articles, and press releases by translating them into Turkish or presenting them in an explanatory manner.
    • We aim to make science accessible to visually impaired individuals by narrating the content we produce.
    • We help high school and even middle school students develop an interest in science and shape their career plans around it. To achieve this, we create content, publish informative editions, and produce videos. Additionally, we answer questions from young people.
    • We aim to visit areas with limited access to science and technology, deliver presentations to the youth there, and contribute to broadening their horizons.

    Sci-Hook Organization

    May 2023 - Present
    Ankara, TR

    Sci-Hook Organization is a global organization that works to bring science to more people and develops various scientific tools for academic work. Currently, it continues its activities with its volunteers. Its goal is to increase the number of projects and expand its impact.

    • I led the establishment of the organization and am currently actively involved in its management processes.
    • I took on the role of founder for the Bilimetri project under the organization's umbrella.
